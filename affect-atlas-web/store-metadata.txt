APP NAME: AffectAtlas

SHORT DESCRIPTION (80 chars max):
Transform emotions into complete design systems with typography, color and motion

FULL DESCRIPTION:
AffectAtlas turns emotions into production-ready design systems.

Select any of 29 emotions on Plutchik's Wheel and watch the entire interface transform -- colors, typography, spacing, motion, shadows, depth and shapes all shift to embody that emotion. Then dive into a deep design advisory with research-backed guidance on how to apply emotional design in your own projects.

What you get for each emotion:
-- Complete color system (primary, secondary, accent, surface, background, text + 5-step palette)
-- Curated font pairing (headline + body) from Google Fonts
-- Spacing scale with density guidance
-- Motion character with easing curves and interactive demos
-- Shadow and depth system
-- Shape language with border radius and corner style
-- Design guidance with creative north star, philosophy, dos and donts
-- Thesaurus word cloud for mood boarding

Export your design system in 9 production formats:
-- CSS custom properties
-- JSON tokens
-- Swift (iOS/macOS)
-- Kotlin (Jetpack Compose)
-- AI prompt (paste into any AI tool)
-- Figma (Tokens Studio plugin)
-- Tailwind CSS config
-- SCSS variables
-- W3C Design Tokens (DTCG standard)

29 emotions across three intensity levels (mild, moderate, intense) means 87 unique design configurations. Love includes Passion and Lust variants with completely different design languages.

Built for designers, brand strategists, therapists, educators and anyone who works with emotional communication.
